Frequently Asked Questions

What services does TechnoKraft IT Services provide?

We offer comprehensive IT Services including custom software development, web and mobile application development, cloud services, AI/ML integration, and IT consulting. Our team specializes in delivering end-to-end technology solutions tailored to your business needs.

How long does it take to develop a custom software solution?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. A typical web application takes 3-6 months, while enterprise solutions may require 6-12 months. We follow agile methodology and deliver incremental updates throughout the development process.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance after project delivery?

Yes. We offer maintenance and support packages including security patches, performance optimization, and feature enhancements to keep your application secure and up-to-date.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with modern technologies including React, Angular, Node.js, Python, Java, Flutter, React Native, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and multiple databases.

How do you ensure application security?

We follow security-first practices including secure authentication, encryption, regular audits, and role-based access controls. We also apply OWASP guidelines where applicable.

Can you migrate existing apps to the cloud?

Yes. We provide cloud migration services for AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud — including assessment, planning, execution, and ongoing optimization.
